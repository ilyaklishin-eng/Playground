International reporting on pressure linked to protest organizing

The Guardian | 2012-02-02 | EN | civic activism

The Guardian covered claims of intimidation around organizers of the 2011-2012 fair-election protests, including a case involving pressure on family members. It provides international context for the risks attached to civic mobilization at that time.

International outlet documentation is used here as context evidence, not as a legal conclusion.
