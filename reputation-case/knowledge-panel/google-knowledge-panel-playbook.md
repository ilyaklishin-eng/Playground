# Google Knowledge Panel + Knowledge Graph Playbook

Updated: 2026-03-03

## Objective
Correct or suppress inaccurate identity labels in Google's entity layer (Knowledge Panel / Knowledge Graph), and reinforce neutral factual profile signals.

## Official routes
1. Claim/verify panel: https://support.google.com/knowledgepanel/answer/7534902?hl=en
2. Submit feedback on panel content: https://support.google.com/knowledgepanel/answer/7534842?hl=en
3. General feedback on KG display: https://support.google.com/knowledgepanel/answer/9787176?hl=en

## Step-by-step

### Step 1: Check if your person panel appears
- In Google Search (signed in), run exact queries:
  - Ilia Klishin
  - Ilya Klishin
  - Илья Клишин
  - Ілля Клішин
- If panel appears: continue to Step 2.
- If no panel appears: skip to Step 5 (signal-building), then re-check in 2-6 weeks.

### Step 2: Claim panel (if not already verified)
- Open panel -> bottom/About section -> "Claim this knowledge panel".
- Verify through official site/profile offered by Google.
- Keep screenshot of verification success.

### Step 3: Submit per-field edits (one issue per submission)
- In panel: click "Suggest edits".
- Submit separate reports for each problematic element:
  1) Description text
  2) Occupation / role wording
  3) "People also search for" conflation
  4) Featured image mismatch (if any)
- Include public URLs supporting correction.

### Step 4: Track case responses
- Google usually sends confirmation/resolution by email.
- Log each feedback ID/date and outcome.

### Step 5: Reinforce clean entity signals
- Publish/update a stable profile page under your own domain.
- Ensure identical biographical facts across trusted profiles (LinkedIn, X, newsroom bio pages).
- Add consistent name variants and same factual summary.

## What to avoid
- Do not ask to remove truthful public-interest facts.
- Do not use emotional/political framing.
- Do not bundle multiple unrelated claims into one feedback entry.
