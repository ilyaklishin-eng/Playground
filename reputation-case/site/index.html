Likes Go West

Vedomosti • 2014-05-21

In this May 21, 2014 Vedomosti column, Klishin argued that coordinated online manipulation techniques tested in Russia could be redirected toward Western audiences and political campaigns. He described an emerging operational mindset: escalation through provocative messaging, synthetic engagement, and cross-platform amplification rather than open persuasion.

Mass protests: lessons from a decade

The Moscow Times • 2021-12-10

In this Dec 10, 2021 Moscow Times essay, Klishin revisits the 2011-2012 protest wave and questions the binary verdict that the movement simply failed. He traces how mass street mobilization shifted political language, expectations, and civic habits even without immediate institutional change.

Putin-Macron talks and the Russia expertise gap

The Moscow Times • 2022-02-14

In this Feb 14, 2022 Moscow Times opinion piece, Ilya Klishin argues that a small factual error in foreign reporting around Putin-Macron talks exposed a larger expertise problem in commentary about Russia.
