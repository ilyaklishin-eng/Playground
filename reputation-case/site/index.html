Mass protests: lessons from a decade

The Moscow Times • 2021-12-10

10, 2021 Moscow Times article revisits the 2011-2012 fair-elections protests a decade later and asks whether the movement truly "failed" or instead reshaped the political field in slower ways. Klishin traces the sequence from mass mobilization to repression: criminal cases, police violence, pressure on media owners, and harassment of public figures.
