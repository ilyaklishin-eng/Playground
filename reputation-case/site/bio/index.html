Ilia Klishin (Ilya Klishin)

Ilia Klishin is a Russian-language journalist, editor, media strategist, and public commentator. This page combines a neutral Wikipedia baseline with source-linked career context from publications and profiles already indexed in this website.

Vilnius, Lithuania · ilyaklishin@gmail.com

Professional profile

Ilia S. Klishin is a media scholar-practitioner with 15+ years of work at the intersection of journalism, digital strategy, and political communication. His practical profile combines newsroom leadership, media product development, and communications architecture for independent civic organizations.

Selected milestones

  • Co-founder and editor-in-chief, Volna Media (2022-present), operating across multiple countries.
  • Author-reported Volna metrics: 50,000+ subscribers and about 2M monthly views.
  • Founder and principal strategist, KF Agency (2017-present), media and communications systems design.
  • Digital Director, RTVI (2016-2019), responsible for digital transformation and audience growth.
  • Head of Digital Newsroom, TV Rain (Dozhd) (2013-2016), during a high-pressure media period.
  • Digital coordinator, Fair Elections movement (2011-2012), protest coordination infrastructure.

Research focus

His current research agenda studies how modern Russian propaganda combines Soviet legacy, 1990s black-PR methods, and corporate marketing technologies. A second axis is media engineering in exile: building resilient information models for fragmented diaspora audiences.

Honours and public work

  • Featured by The Guardian in the New East "30 under 30" list (2015).
  • Regular contributor across The Moscow Times, Vedomosti, and Carnegie platforms.
  • Public speaker on media systems, including TEDx-related appearances.

Education and languages

  • MGIMO University: BA and MA in International Relations (EU studies), 2004-2010.
  • Aristotle University of Thessaloniki: international exchange/internship track.
  • FLEX alumnus: one academic year in the United States (2003-2004).
  • Languages: Russian (native), English (C2), Lithuanian (B1), Modern Greek (B1), German (A2).

Personal details provided by Ilia Klishin for this page: married since 2017; father of two daughters, Zoya (born 2020) and Sofia (born 2025).
