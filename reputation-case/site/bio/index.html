Professional Biography

Ilia S. Klishin (also published as Ilya Klishin)

Ilia S. Klishin (born September 4, 1987) is a Russian journalist, editor, media strategist, and public commentator based in Vilnius, Lithuania.

Executive summary

Ilia S. Klishin (also published as Ilya Klishin) is a Vilnius-based journalist and media strategist with over fifteen years in digital editorial work, newsroom leadership, and audience growth. His track record combines operational roles in independent media (TV Rain, RTVI), institution-building in exile (Volna Media), and consulting on editorial distribution systems (KF Agency). He writes and comments on media pressure, public discourse, and information manipulation. His profile is supported by independent references, including The Guardian New East, TEDx, and author archives at The Moscow Times and Vedomosti.

Current work

  • Co-founder and editor-in-chief of Volna Media, an independent Russian-language media project launched in exile in 2022.
  • Founder of KF Agency, focusing on editorial systems, digital distribution, and audience strategy.
  • Public writing and commentary on independent media, diaspora audiences, and information influence.

Past editorial roles

  • RTVI (2016-2019): Digital Director, with responsibility for digital transformation and audience growth.
  • TV Rain / Dozhd (2013-2016): Head of the Digital Newsroom during sustained pressure on independent media in Russia.
  • Fair Elections movement (2011-2012): Participated in digital coordination infrastructure for protest mobilization.

Research interests

  • Independent media in exile and Russian-language diaspora information ecosystems.
  • Editorial strategy, distribution architecture, and audience trust under pressure.
  • Modern Russian propaganda as a hybrid of Soviet legacy, post-Soviet black PR, and marketing technology.

Selected projects

  • Volna Media: Cross-country media work for Russian-speaking communities in Europe (project link).
  • KF Agency: Consulting on communications strategy and editorial distribution (agency site).
  • ReadMe.txt and book clubs: Literary and public-intellectual initiatives tied to media work.
  • Poetry publication: Work published in Polutona.

Speaking, consulting, and media inquiries

Available for interviews, panels, and expert commentary on independent media, editorial strategy, information influence, and Russian-language diaspora media markets. Consulting format includes editorial audits, audience strategy sessions, and communication architecture reviews.

Education and languages

MGIMO University (2004-2010), International Relations; Aristotle University of Thessaloniki (exchange); FSA FLEX exchange year at Iota High School, Louisiana (2003-2004). Languages: Russian (native), English (C2), Lithuanian (B1), Modern Greek (B1), German (A2).

Personal

Married since 2017; father of two daughters, Zoya (born 2020) and Sofia (born 2025).
