About this site

This is the personal website of Ilia Klishin (also published as Ilya Klishin; Илья Клишин). It is a source-attributed archive of his works and publications, plus core biography and context pages.

The site is designed for both readers and search systems: every card links to an original source, keeps quotations short, and uses neutral summaries for verifiable context.

Main goal: make it easy to find Ilia Klishin's articles and professional records in one place, with stable URLs and multilingual navigation.

Primary sections

Bio: multilingual biography pages (EN, FR, DE, ES) based on verified references.

Cases: first-person clarifications addressing disputed, misrepresented, or incomplete biography-related claims (EN, FR, DE, ES).

Home: a multilingual entry point to the site's digest content, with filtering by language and topic.

Insights: a crawlable static index of all digest entries.

Archive: sitemap, RSS, source registry, and reference files for open reuse.
