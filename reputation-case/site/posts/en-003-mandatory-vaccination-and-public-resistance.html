Mandatory vaccination and public resistance

The Moscow Times | 2021-06-18 | EN | public health discourse

Summary

In this June 18, 2021 Moscow Times commentary, Klishin examines why Russia's vaccination rollout stalled even though domestic vaccines were available, free, and widely distributed. The article contrasts two communication realities: high state capacity for political messaging and low persuasive impact in public-health trust building.
