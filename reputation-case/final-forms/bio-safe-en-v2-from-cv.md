# Safe Bio (EN) - Source-Backed Version

Ilia S. Klishin is a media practitioner working across digital journalism and strategic communications.
He has worked in editorial and digital leadership roles connected with TV Rain and RTVI, and has published in outlets including Vedomosti, The Moscow Times, and Snob.

Klishin was included in The Guardian New East "30 under 30: Moscow's young power list" (2015).
Public reporting also links him to the digital coordination environment of the 2011-2012 fair-elections protest wave, including the Big White Circle action.

In 2022, he co-founded Volna, a media project oriented toward Russian-speaking communities in migration.
His current focus combines practical media-building experience with research interests in propaganda systems, digital autocracy, and media engineering.
