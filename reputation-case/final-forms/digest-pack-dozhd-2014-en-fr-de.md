## EN (ready)

### Title
2014: TV Rain launched a public investigation into Russian soldiers in Donbas

### Body
In August 2014, TV Rain launched a civic investigation project ("Nashi Soldaty") to verify reports about Russian servicemen killed or captured near the Ukrainian border during a period of official denials.

The project used open evidence collection and case-by-case verification, including reader submissions, field reporting, and cross-checking of public claims.

Im August 2014 startete TV Rain das Projekt "Nashi Soldaty", um Berichte ueber getoetete oder gefangene russische Soldaten nahe der ukrainischen Grenze in einer Phase offizieller Dementis zu verifizieren.
